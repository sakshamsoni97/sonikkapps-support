Frequently Asked Questions

Thank you for checking our Frequently Asked Questions section, more FAQs will be added as we hear more from our users.

What devices are supported?

This app is currently on supported on Apple Watch devices running WatchOS 26 or higher.

Is there a free tier for the app?

Currently, due to limited resources, we only have a single paid tier.

Does the app collect my data?

We don't collect any personal indentifiable information, but we may collect anonymized usage data to understand how our users engage with the app to improve it.
